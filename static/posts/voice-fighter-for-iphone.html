Voice Fighter (for iPhone)

Play the game only by your VOICE !!!

Voice Fighter is a new voice control shooting game!

  2. Shout “Pah” to shoot.

  3. Shout for two seconds to launch the ultimate bomb!!!

MORE fun! MORE crazier!
